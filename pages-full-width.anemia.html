    ANEMIA


    HOME REMEDIES

    • Epsom Salt : Take a bath with warm water mixed with Epsom salt. You can also soak your feet, if you wish so.
    • Iron utensils : Cook your food in iron vessels and pots on a daily basis as it can combat the problem of iron deficiency by increasing the iron in your food.
    • Massage : Getting a whole body massage with oils, like mustard, olive or coconut can help in increasing the blood circulation of the body.
    • Tomato Juice : Imbibe the juice of tomato mixed with apple juice, daily, to alleviate anaemia.
    • Banana : Consume a ripe banana with two tablespoons honey, every day. It is a natural remedy for overcoming anaemia.
    • Almonds : Soak about 6-7 almonds in water for a night and eat it the next morning, after peeling its skin.




    HEALTH TIPS

    • You can help prevent iron-deficiency anemia by eating a well-balanced diet that includes good sources of iron, vitamin B12, and folate.
    • Select iron-fortified cereals and breads.
    • Carefully follow safety guidelines if your occupation involves work with lead-containing materials such as batteries, petroleum, and paint.
    • If you are a vegetarian or vegan, talk to your doctor or a nutritionist about your diet and any possible need for supplements.
