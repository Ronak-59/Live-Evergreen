Description

This site was developed as an outcome of a hackathon competition organised by KJSCE Abhiyantriki towards 'Solution that makes life simpler for the aged'. It aims to provide entertainment like reading newspaper, listening songs, playing games and even instant home remedies for the most common diseases faced by the older people. This site helps to enhance the over-all interaction of the aged with the technology to make the most out of it.
