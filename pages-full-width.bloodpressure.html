HOME REMEDIES

  • Lemon Juice : Mainly when you are suffering from dehydration. So treat your low blood pressure, mix lemon juice with water and add a teaspoon of salt stir it and drink.
  • Saltwater : Salt helps to treat low blood pressure because the sodium helps to increase the blood pressure. So for this remedy what you need to do is simply mix one and a half teaspoon of salt in a glass of water and mix it well and then drink it
  • Coffee : Having a strong cup of coffee daily may also help to increase your blood pressure. If you suffer from low blood pressure then drink a cup of coffee in the morning and also have it along with your meals
  • Raisins : It is a very easy home remedy to cure hypotension naturally. Just soak a handful of raisins in a cup of water overnight and eat them in the morning on empty stomach and drink water in which the raisins were soaked.
  • Yoghurt : Have one cup of yoghurt daily in your breakfast. Yogurt consists of calcium, magnesium and potassium that is very important for our daily life. So have this yogurt as your salad dressing, you can also have it with your fruit salad.
